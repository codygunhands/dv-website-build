Privacy, in plain English.

We collect what we need to keep our apps working and improving. We don't sell your data. Here's the long version.

The short version

We don’t run user accounts. We don’t sell your data. We don’t use ad trackers. Your calculation inputs and results never leave your device. The apps do send a small amount of anonymous diagnostic data to help us catch crashes and understand which Pro features matter most — details below.

Our calculator apps (ShopCalc, WelderCalc, BuilderCalc, ElectricianCalc)

All calculation work happens entirely on your device. Your inputs, results, and saved data are never transmitted anywhere.

The apps include a lightweight anonymous diagnostic layer that sends two categories of events to our own server (support.doublevision.company):

  • Crash & error reports — when JavaScript throws an unexpected error, a report is sent containing the error message, a stack trace, the app version, and the device platform (iOS/Android). No personally identifiable information is included.
  • Paywall interaction events — when you tap a Pro-gated feature or the “Unlock Pro” button, we record which feature you tapped (e.g. “tig”, “framing”), the app version, and the platform. This helps us understand which features drive the most value. No inputs, results, account info, or identifiers are attached.

These events are anonymous — we have no way to tie them back to you as an individual. We retain them for up to 90 days for debugging and product decisions, then they’re deleted.

The optional Pro subscription or one-time unlock is processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). They handle the payment; we receive a payout, not your card details.

Our websites (doublevision.company, dutydesk.doublevision.company, nomad.doublevision.company)

doublevision.company and nomad.doublevision.company are static marketing pages served through a CDN. We don’t set tracking cookies, we don’t run third-party advertising tags, and we don’t have a session/login system on those pages. Standard hosting access logs (IP address, user agent, requested URL, timestamp) are kept short-term by our hosting provider for operational purposes.

DutyDesk (dutydesk.doublevision.company) is a separate web app with its own account system. What it collects, how long it keeps it, and your choices are covered in the DutyDesk privacy policy at dutydesk.doublevision.company/privacy.

Contact & inquiry forms

The Ace & Alan and Custom Software inquiry forms on doublevision.company open your email client with a pre-filled message addressed to us. When you hit send, your email provider delivers it to our inbox. We see whatever you put in that email and we keep it as long as we need it to do business with you.

Phone & email

If you call or email us we keep normal business records of the conversation (your phone number, email address, what we discussed) so we can follow up and so we have a record if there’s a dispute later. We don’t use it for marketing.

What we don’t do

  • We don’t sell your data. Not now, not later.
  • We don’t collect your name, email, location, or any personally identifiable information in the apps.
  • We don’t run advertising/retargeting trackers (Google Ads, Meta Pixel, etc.).
  • We don’t build user profiles or share your contact info with third parties for marketing.

Service providers we use

The handful of vendors that touch any of this:

  • DigitalOcean — web hosting for our marketing sites, inquiry-form delivery, and the anonymous diagnostic endpoint the apps report to.
  • Cloudflare — CDN and basic DDoS protection in front of those sites.
  • Apple App Store — distributes the iOS apps and processes in-app purchases.
  • Google Play — distributes the Android apps and processes in-app purchases.
  • Our email provider — receives and stores email you send us through forms or directly.

We’ll update this list before we add any vendor that meaningfully touches your data.

Your rights

The diagnostic events we collect are anonymous — there’s nothing to link back to you. If you’ve emailed us or sent an inquiry through one of our forms and you want us to delete what we have, email privacy@doublevision.company and we will. We respond inside 30 days.

Children

Our products aren’t directed at anyone under 16. We don’t knowingly collect data from minors.

Changes

If we change this policy materially, we’ll update the effective date at the top of this page.
