What it does

Built around the problem, not around a roadmap.

The product solves something specific and gets out of your way.

  • 01

    Step-by-step project flows - five guided walkthroughs

    Tap a project. Answer 2–3 simple questions. BuilderCalc walks you from inputs → humanized result → shopping list → mistakes to avoid → done. Free flows: Paint a room · Tile a bathroom floor. Pro flows: Build a deck · Install drywall · Pour a concrete slab. Each step shows progress (Step 3 of 5), each result shows a pro tip, and the whole thing is built like a checklist instead of a wall of text.

  • 02

    Humanized output - never just a raw number

    Other calculators say "2.4 gallons." BuilderCalc says "Buy 3 gallons (rounded safe so you don't run out mid-wall) - estimated cost $119–$168." Every result card includes the math (wall area, coats, coverage), the rounded shopping number, the cost band, and a pro tip you can use right now. No more standing in aisle 7 doing mental arithmetic.

  • 03

    Shopping list - wired to your inputs

    Every project ends with a tap-to-check shopping list scoped to your dimensions and choices. Picked premium one-coat paint? You see Aura/Emerald/Marquee, Wooster Shortcut, microfiber rollers, and FrogTape. Picked Schluter Ditra for tile? You see Ditra membrane, modified thinset, leveling clips, and the right trowel notch. Tap items off as you grab them. Knows what to buy, in the order you'll grab it.

  • 04

    Mistakes to Avoid - the brutal sourced list

    Mesh tape in inside drywall corners. Lag bolts in a single row on a deck ledger. Bull-floating concrete with bleed water on top. Painting silicone caulk. Garage ceiling drywall not 5/8″ Type X. 27 mistakes that cost DIYers thousands every year, each tied to the IRC / ANSI / TCNA section or the pro-consensus reason it fails.

  • 05

    Materials reference - when to use what (and what NOT to use)

    32 brand-grouped cards. Wood (Huber AdvanTech, Baltic birch B/BB, KDAT, UC4A). Fasteners (GRK R4, SPAX, SDWS, TOGGLER SnapToggle's 238 lb rating). Adhesives (Titebond III, Loctite PL Premium, JB Weld). Sealants (Sashco Big Stretch for siding, OSI Quad MAX for Hardie, GE Supreme for tubs, Sikaflex-1a for masonry). Each card: when to use, when NOT to use, what plays well with it. Stop guessing.

  • 06

    Paint & Finishes - the prep details that separate pros

    Brand picks first (Benjamin Moore Aura, Sherwin-Williams Emerald, Behr Marquee - true one-coat in same-color repaints). Wooster Shortcut for cut-in. Floetrol additive for self-leveling. Zinsser BIN for stain-blocking. Sheen by where, not by look. Drying & recoat windows. The pro tricks: box your paint, strain through a stocking, fridge the brush overnight, holiday-hunt with raking light.

  • 07

    14 calculators - still in seconds, when you just need the math

    Concrete (slab/footing/column/pier). Beam Span (L/360 + L/240). Joist Spacing. Wall Framing. Rafter. Roof + Shingles. Stairs (IRC R311.7). Drywall. Paint. Insulation. Rebar (ASTM A615). Deck Materials. Pressure Loss (Hazen-Williams). Reference. All open instantly, all imperial + metric, all wired into the project flows so you can drill back into the math when you want it.

  • 08

    Standards under the hood - IRC, ACI, ANSI, TCNA, NDS

    Deck spans + cantilever from IRC R507 (with the 2021 R507.6 update). Stair rise/run from R311.7. Drywall thickness from R702.3.5 + garage R302.6 (5/8″ Type X). Concrete from ACI 318 + 308. Tile mortar coverage from ANSI A108.5. Lumber section properties from NDS. Sourced, cited, in your pocket - even when service isn't.

  • 09

    Pro Unlock - three ways to get it

    Pick the fit. $3.99/mo to try, $28.99/yr for the best value (saves ~40% over monthly), or $64.99 once - pay once, every future Pro feature included automatically. Original Pro buyers from earlier versions automatically own Lifetime. No ads, no accounts, no tracking.
