What it does

Built around the problem, not around a roadmap.

The product solves something specific and gets out of your way.

  • 01

    Cones & frustums

    Unfold a cone or frustum (full 360°, half, or quarter wrap) into its gore - small/large radius, included angle, slant length, and arc lengths, all computed for you. Eccentric and oblique cones too.

  • 02

    Cylinders, half-sleeves & ducts

    Rectangular blanks for rolled cylinders and wraps, with developed circumference and seam allowance, plus square-to-round duct panels with bend lines.

  • 03

    Real sheet-metal development

    Thickness + K-factor aware - true neutral-axis development with OD / ID / MEAN / neutral-axis diameter resolution. The bend allowance is right, so the part comes out to size, not 'close enough.'

  • 04

    1:1 layered DXF export

    Export a 1:1 DXF (R12) with CUT, BEND, ROLL and centerline on separate layers - drops straight onto a plasma, laser, or waterjet table. Free DXF export, no watermark.

  • 05

    Mark-out numbers for hand layout

    Every pattern comes with the swing radius, included angle, arc lengths and chords grouped into Layout / Mark-out / Reference - everything you need to strike it on the bench with a trammel and a tape.

  • 06

    PDF job sheets & saved patterns (Pro)

    Save a pattern to recall later, and print a clean PDF job sheet with the drawing and the numbers for the guy at the table. No accounts, works offline.

  • 07

    Pro Unlock - three ways to get it.

    Pick the fit. $4.99/mo to try, $29.99/yr for the best value, or $59.99 once - pay once, every future Pro feature included. No ads, no accounts, no tracking. Works the same in a shop with no Wi-Fi as the day you installed it.
